First Aid Guide

Learn basic first aid techniques to handle emergencies.

Basic First Aid Guide

  • CPR: 30 chest compressions followed by 2 rescue breaths.
  • Choking: Perform the Heimlich maneuver.
  • Bleeding: Apply direct pressure and elevate the wound.
  • Burns: Cool under running water for 10-15 minutes.
  • Fractures: Immobilize the injured area.
  • Heat Exhaustion: Move to a cooler place and hydrate.
  • Hypothermia: Warm the person gradually.
  • Seizures: Clear the area and place the person on their side.
  • Poisoning: Call poison control immediately.
