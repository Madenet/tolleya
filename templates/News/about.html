Who We Are

Bridging Communities Through Digital Innovation

Born in Elim, Shihlobyeni, we're a passionate team building digital bridges for our community

At Tolleya News, we develop cutting-edge platforms that connect our communities, making life easier by providing instant access to news, sports, events, and essential updates. Our mission is to empower Limpopo residents with technology that informs, engages, and unites.

  • Local-first approach: Designed by locals, for locals
  • Mobile-optimized platforms accessible to everyone
  • Real-time updates on news, sports, and community events
  • Multilingual support for inclusive access
  • Community-driven content creation
