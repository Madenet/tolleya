New Community Center Opens in Shihlobyeni

  • Thabo Mokoena
  • June 15, 2024
  • 24 Comments
  • 1,245 Views

The community of Shihlobyeni celebrated the grand opening of its new multipurpose center this past weekend. The facility, funded through a partnership between local government and community contributions, will serve as a hub for education, social gatherings, and skills development programs.

Local dignitaries including Mayor Ndlovu and Chief Mabunda attended the ribbon-cutting ceremony, emphasizing the importance of community spaces for social cohesion and youth development. "This center represents our commitment to building a brighter future together," said Mayor Ndlovu.

"When we invest in our community spaces, we invest in our children's future. This center will be a place of learning, celebration, and growth for generations to come."

Chief Mabunda at the opening ceremony

The two-story building features a computer lab, meeting rooms, a kitchen, and a large hall that can accommodate up to 200 people. Construction began in January and was completed ahead of schedule thanks to volunteer labor from community members.

Community Impact

Already, several groups have scheduled use of the facility. The local women's cooperative will hold sewing classes, the youth forum plans career guidance workshops, and the elders council will host monthly community meetings. A schedule will be posted at the center and on the Tolleya community app.

Future Plans

The management committee is exploring partnerships with NGOs to offer adult education classes and health screenings. "We're just getting started," said committee chairperson Mrs. Khumalo. "This building is just the shell - it's the people and programs that will bring it to life."

Volunteers are needed for various roles at the center. Those interested can sign up at the facility or through the Tolleya platform.
